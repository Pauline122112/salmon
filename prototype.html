Bird Nerd

Y / 2016

TYPE / Personal Project + Proof of concept

SERVICES / UX/UI + PROTOTYPE

Young leaders is a programme sponsored by the Ministry of Primary Industries. It encourages internal staff to submit ideas in areas of technology and design. Bird Nerd was an idea pitched by a small team. The small team need some concepts and prototypes to pitch.

Process

scoping

Using sound recognition technology, bird nerd is an app that helps users identify native New Zealand birds while out in the forest. Bird Nerd also allows users to tag and share their location and add birds to their personal collection. The app would generally be targeted towards bird enthusiast but also needed to appeal other target groups. The team at MPI needed a prototype to present to.
