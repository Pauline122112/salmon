Bird Nerd

Y / 2016

TYPE / Personal Project + Proof of concept

SERVICES / UX/UI + PROTOTYPE

Young leaders is a programme sponsored by the Ministry of Primary Industries. It encourages internal staff to submit ideas in areas of technology and design. Bird Nerd was an idea pitched by a small team. The small team need some concepts and prototypes to pitch.

Process

scoping

Using sound recognition technology, bird nerd is an app that helps users identify native New Zealand birds while out in the forest. Bird Nerd also allows users to tag and share their location and add birds to their personal collection. The app would generally be targeted towards bird enthusiast but also needed to appeal other target groups. The team at MPI needed a prototype to present to.

Early sketches provided by team at MPI

Wireframing

For this case study I choose to develop one user story, On-boarding

pen and paper

Based on the sketches I received from the stakeholders, I spent approx an hour producing my own wireframes.

Sketch app

Once I got a general idea of what elements were needed for this prototype, I then moved the wireframing into SKETCH for more precise wireframing to help communicate more complex interactions and flows. Sketch UI kits allowed me quickly produce vector based wireframe which I could then easily share and receive feedback.

MOCKUPS

Using a combination of sketch and photoshop, the wireframes were then converted into high-fidelity mockups with color, typography and other visual elements.
