What's the process?

We expect everyone to participate, be committed and work hard. In return, we will open up our network to you and actively support you for 12 months.

You will get access to workshops, network, investors, advise, data science labs (AI/IoT etc), workshops, social events and – of course – office and meeting room facilities.

We talk to your team and consumers, research competitors and learn everything about the market. We then take your brand's unique position in the market and craft a story around it to enable awesome.

Scoping

Using sound recognition technology, bird nerd is an app that helps users identify native New Zealand birds while out in the forest. Bird Nerd also allows users to tag and share their location and add birds to their personal collection. The app would generally be targeted towards bird enthusiast but also needed to appeal other target groups. The team at MPI needed a prototype to present to.

(Early sketches provided by team at MPI)

Wireframing

For this case study I choose to develop one user story, On-boarding. A website wireframe, also known as a page schematic or screen blueprint, is a visual guide that represents the skeletal framework of a website. :166. Wireframes are created for the purpose of arranging elements to best accomplish a particular purpose.

Pen and paper

Based on the sketches I received from the stakeholders, I spent approx an hour producing my own wireframes. Below show my initial sketches

Using Sketch app to produce higher fidelity

Once I got a general idea of what elements were needed for this prototype, I then moved the wireframing into SKETCH for more precise wireframing to help communicate more complex interactions and flows. Sketch UI kits allowed me quickly produce vector based wireframe which I could then easily share and receive feedback.
