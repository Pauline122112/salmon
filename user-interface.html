Connecting travellers around New Zealand

For our final week at Enspiral Dev Academy, each student was required to pitch an idea for final group projects in which we can 6 days to complete. Our team consisted of 6 members and the chosen idea was Tandem. Tandem is a ridesharing app that connects drivers and passengers willing to travel together and share the costs of travel. The app was built using Javascript, Node.js and PostgreSQL. My role was to design and front end. I produced wireframes, user-interface and coded most of the CSS.
