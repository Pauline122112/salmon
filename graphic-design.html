Introduction

For our final week at Enspiral Dev Academy, each student was required to pitch an idea for final group projects, each team had six days to complete the project.

Our team consisted of 6 members and the chosen idea was Tandem. Tandem is a ridesharing app that connects drivers and passengers willing to travel together and share the costs of travel. Its aimed at drivers travelling long distances looking to fill empty seats and reducing the costs of travel.

Every team member was involved with product development and planning. My Role was to design the user interface and front end development. Based on the produced wireframes I implemented the visual elements and layouts.
